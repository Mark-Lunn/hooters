Quandoo Policy

We use Quandoo to handle our online and offline reservations for us. Quandoo is a restaurant technology platform that provides the ability for us to accept, create and manage reservations along with the associated personal customer data to better enable us to concentrate on your dining experience. Quandoo uses cookies and other technologies to collect and process the data relating to reservations and visitor behaviour when using the booking functionality on this website. Quandoo securely stores this information on our behalf.
